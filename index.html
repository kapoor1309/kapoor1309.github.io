About

I'm a pre-final year undergraduate at IIT Roorkee, focused on building AI systems that are robust, interpretable, and useful in the real world. My work spans medical imaging, reasoning in vision-language models through scene-graph generation, and autonomous AI agents.

I've co-authored papers at AAAI (Student Abstract Track) and ICVGIP 2025, and designed and deployed multi-agent systems during my AI/ML internship at WhyMinds Global. I enjoy taking ideas from a paper to a working, dependable pipeline.
